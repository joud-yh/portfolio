SEAبيئة مستدامة

معاً نحو بيئة مستدامة

Sustainable Environmental Awareness — one Arabic-first platform that gathers Saudi Arabia's environmental initiatives, teaches, and hands people the tools to act.

🌍
Overview

Protecting the environment starts with commitment· نبذة

Across Saudi Arabia, environmental effort is real but scattered — campaigns here, regulations there, initiatives somewhere else. SEA brings them under one calm, Arabic-first roof: a place to learn, to take part, and to understand the rules that protect the land.

The premise

A single platform that enhances environmental awareness and encourages sustainable practices in the Kingdom. It gathers initiatives, delivers educational and interactive content, and gives users tools to contribute — all reachable from home, school, or work, on any device, at any time.

My part of the team was the human side: mapping how a real user feels using the site, then designing the web pages they move through.

The shift

From scattered effort to one shared home

People care about the environment — they just don't know where to start, what's official, or how to help.

The gap

Environmental initiatives, educational material, and compliance rules live in separate places. For an ordinary person, that means no clear front door — and no obvious way to take part.

"I wanted to help, but I didn't know where to even look."

The promise

One platform that explains, invites participation, and surfaces the laws — calm Arabic content, interactive tools, and a layout simple enough that the important information is always within reach.

"It's simple — and I can find what matters."

The idea

Three jobs, one platform

🌱

Gather initiatives

Bring scattered environmental efforts and initiatives together under one platform.

📖

Educate & engage

Provide educational and interactive content that makes sustainability easy to understand.

🛠️

Tools to act

Give users tools to contribute to protecting the environment — and to check compliance.

Challenges

The hard parts· التحديات

01

Pinning down the concept

Our biggest challenge was defining the idea precisely. The scope kept widening, so we returned to the primary goal — awareness and sustainable practice — and let every decision serve that.

02

Unifying scattered initiatives

Many separate efforts had to feel like one place. We grouped them into four clear destinations so a visitor never feels lost between sections.

03

Making it feel Saudi

Generic green design wasn't enough. We leaned on local imagery and Vision 2030 language so the platform reads as authentically Saudi.

04

Depth without clutter

Education needs detail; awareness needs calm. We kept pages spacious and surfaced the key information first, with depth a click away.
