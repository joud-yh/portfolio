Habity

Hapity is an application to build a better life for the user by building a routine by applying the habits attached to the user’s chosen plan in addition to the habits he wants/needs.
The main vision of Habity is to improve the user’s routine to suit his goals and motivate him to stick to them.
The application motivates and encourages the user to adhere to habits and provides a community for users to communicate, encourage each other, and share their achievements.
