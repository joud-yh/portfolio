Habityهابتي

A habit-building app that turns your goals into daily routines — and routines into a community that keeps you going.

🌱
Overview

Build the routine, keep the habit· نبذة

Habity helps people build a better life by turning goals into routines — pairing the habits from a chosen plan with the ones you add yourself, then keeping you motivated to stick with them.

The premise

You pick a plan that fits your personality and goals, Habity arranges it into morning, afternoon and evening routines, and a built-in community lets users encourage each other and share what they've achieved.

The whole project ran a full UX process — research, personas, journeys, information architecture, wireframes, UI and a documented style guide — across 14 screens and 336 hours.

The shift

From good intentions to real routines

People rarely fail at habits because they don't care — they fail because building and sticking to a routine alone is hard, and motivation fades.

The problem

Sticking to it, alone, is hard

Setting a goal is easy; holding a routine isn't. Without structure and encouragement, new habits quietly slip away.

Habity's promise

A routine that fits — and a crowd that cheers

Personalized routines built from a plan, gentle reminders, a visible journey, and a community that celebrates every small win.
